About DeTLeng
=========================================================

DeTLeng is an AI and Data Engineering company focused on transforming business data into trusted intelligence, automation, and measurable business value.

The company specializes in building modern data platforms, analytics systems, Business Intelligence solutions, AI assistants, AI agents, and intelligent business systems.

DeTLeng combines Data Engineering, Analytics Engineering, Business Intelligence, and Artificial Intelligence to help organizations make better decisions from trusted data.

AI Business Intelligence
=========================================================

DeTLeng AI Business Intelligence helps users understand trusted business analytics through natural language.

The platform connects business users with curated analytical data so they can ask questions about revenue, customers, orders, products, sellers, payments, reviews, delivery, and other business KPIs.

The AI Business Intelligence experience is designed to make analytics easier to access, easier to understand, and more useful for decision-making.

=========================================================
Analytics Layer
=========================================================

The Analytics Layer is the trusted source for business KPIs and analytical results.

Business metrics are prepared through data engineering and analytics engineering processes before being exposed to the AI Business Intelligence platform.

The MCP Business Intelligence Server connects the AI layer with approved business analytics capabilities from the Analytics Layer.

This approach helps ensure that business answers are based on modeled, validated, and analytics-ready data.
